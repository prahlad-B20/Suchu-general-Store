How do I place an order? +
Simply add products to your cart, click checkout, fill in your details, and send your order directly to us via WhatsApp. We'll confirm your order right away.

Yes, we deliver within Nagri and nearby areas. Delivery details are confirmed with you over WhatsApp after you place your order.
